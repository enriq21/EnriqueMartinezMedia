The Haircut Thing

I'm really not sure why I do it, to be honest. The first few haircuts were just out of necessity, and timing wise, it just worked out that that we were traveling. Today, its definitely something I'll go out of way to do. In some cases, letting my hair grow a little unruly to give myself a reason to go somewhere new. Unfortunately, I didn't really document the first couple cuts. So far though, the list is still pretty short and I'm excited to keep adding to it.
