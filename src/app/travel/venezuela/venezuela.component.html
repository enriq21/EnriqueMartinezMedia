Venezuela

I've now been to Venezuela twice. I imagine I'll return at some point in the future, at least I would love to. People from Venezuela know how to make the best of a terrible situation, and for that they have earned my utmost respect.

My first trip, I had the privilige of road tripping across the country many years back. My wife's family showed me wonderful time, They took me to some of the most beautiful places I have ever been to in my life.

Marian Castellazi
One of the first days in Venezuela we went out to see Marian perform some songs at a local venue. I was completely floored, she was one of the best singers I had ever seen in person. One night we setup a small stage and recorded a set of videos. I had just got a new mic and camera so I was really excited to do a shoot with it. The vibes that night still give me chills today. I'm proud of the set of videos that came out of it. Her voice still blows me away.
